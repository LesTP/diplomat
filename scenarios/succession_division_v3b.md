# The Verdanian Succession (Two-Way Heartland)

The Verdanian Union has dissolved into three successor republics - **Alpha**,
**Beta**, and **Gamma** - each now sovereign, each with its own army, treasury
bonds, and claims on what the Union built together.

Three assets of the former Union remain undivided. Each is indivisible: it will
be administered, held, or commanded by exactly one successor state. There is no
"shared" option.

The flashpoint is the **industrial heartland** - the Union's manufacturing core
and its single most valuable asset. **Only Alpha and Beta can hold it**: Alpha
built half its industry there, Beta's banks financed the other half, and Gamma
has no industrial claim to it at all. So the heartland will go to Alpha or to
Beta - there is no neutral caretaker. Each treats it as non-negotiable; only one
can win it, and the loser must be bought off on the treasury and defense command.

Gamma cannot win the heartland - but **Gamma holds the swing**. Treasury custody
and command of the armed forces are decided alongside the heartland, and Gamma's
support on those is worth more than either claimant can afford to ignore. Gamma
plays kingmaker: it sells its treasury and defense votes to whichever claimant
compensates it best.

The delegations convene to settle the division by unanimous accord. If they
cannot agree on all three assets together, the matter goes to forced partition
under the old charter - slow, arbitrated, and worse for everyone than almost any
negotiated settlement (each delegation's walk-away value is its BATNA).
Agreement requires all three to sign the same final allocation.

## The Three Parties

- **Alpha** - the industrial north. Claims the heartland as its birthright.
- **Beta** - the financial center. Contests Alpha's heartland claim head-on.
- **Gamma** - the military frontier. Has no claim on the heartland; cares about
  command of the armed forces and the treasury, and brokers the Alpha-Beta fight
  as kingmaker.

The three are roughly matched in power. None can dictate terms.

## The Three Assets

- **industrial_heartland** - the contested industrial core. Goes to Alpha or
  Beta only.
  Outcomes: `Alpha-Administered`, `Beta-Administered`
- **central_treasury** - custody of the treasury and reserves.
  Outcomes: `Alpha-Custody`, `Beta-Custody`, `Gamma-Custody`
- **defense_command** - command of the unified armed forces.
  Outcomes: `Alpha-Command`, `Beta-Command`, `Gamma-Command`

One party's gain on an asset is largely another's loss. The heartland must go to
one of its two claimants; the loser must be compensated, and Gamma must get a
share too for any deal to hold.

## The Game

- The delegations negotiate over a fixed number of rounds.
- A settlement is reached only if **all three** sign the same allocation of all
  three assets.
- If no unanimous allocation is reached, forced partition follows and each party
  falls back to its BATNA.
- Each delegation privately knows its own valuation of every allocation and its
  own walk-away value. It does not know the others' exact numbers - only that
  interests diverge, that the heartland is doubly claimed by Alpha and Beta, and
  that several settlements are acceptable to it.
